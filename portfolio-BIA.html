Escape
NOTE: The following is a map that was cut and never made it out of the pre-alpha stage.

First Courtyard
Players start inside a Mental Hospital/Detention Center built atop old castle ruins on a hilltop. Players have just escaped from captivity amidst riots in the camps below. Searchlights scour the map and German soldiers are running around trying to recapture prisoners and restore order. The first goal (the old ruins at the hill's pinnacle) can clearly be seen from the starting point. Players are thrown into immediate combat, with vehicles, crates and buildings available for cover.
Second Courtyard
As combat and the layout of the map force the Players behind and around the large garage that dominates the entry courtyard, the influx of enemy soldiers increases as the push to reach the ruins at the hilltop, and ultimately the power generator, continues. Soldiers start entering combat by kicking open doors as the Players rush past. Sirens start blaring and searchlights start highlighting the Players for the German soldiers. And again, the hilltop goal is in clear sight.

Shutting Down the Power
The main generator is located at the top of the old castle ruins, nestled among antennas and other generators. Players can either destroy the main generator or pull a switch to shut it down. Either way, Players will get a fantastic view as power-grids go down, one by one, across the lower camps. This also kills the alarms and the searchlights.


After the power-grid has been shut down, prison riots will start up in the lower camp and will be visible from the high vantage point. Players will fight a final battle to escape from the Detention Center and make their way to the Armory Gate in the lower camp. A zip-line at the top of the ruins offers Players the opportunity to avoid a lot of combat and make a quicker exit. Once out of the Detention Center, Players will enter the first of the POW camps and will fight through several block-houses and then a fuel depot with lots of explosives!
Battle on the Upper Slopes
Once past the fuel depot, Players will find themselves fighting down a steep incline full of more block-houses, zip-lines and lots of enemy soldiers. The tower that is the next objective and also the end of the map really starts to come into view at this point, and will remain in view for most of the remainder of the map.
The Lower Camp
Here the enemy soldiers start to get really thick and the difficulty ramps up as the tougher soldiers start showing up and the TNT dogs start appearing as well. At the Armory Gate, located next to the tower that Players have been able to see for quite some time, The Master of the Hounds will make his appearance from the kennels behind the tower. He is a hulking man in heavy armor who raises and trains the TNT dogs. Once he appears the area will start to flood with the TNT dogs who rush Players in suicide rushes. Scripting will control a counter and when enough dogs die, the Master will while for more dogs. Dogs will continue to harass Players until the Master is put down. And the Master also guards the exit through the Armory Gate.
